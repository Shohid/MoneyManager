TimeLine :

This activity will contain graphical representation of your
expenditure till you reset your budget.

Reset Total Budget:

This input box will Reset your budget and your old expenditure
will be safe in the form of a monthly budget.

Update Budget:

This input box will update your budget by adding given
the amount by you.

Money Exchange:

This will contain your lending and borrowing details.

Saving Account:

This input box will Reset your budget and your old expenditure
will be safe in the form of the monthly budget.

 Backup and Restore:

This will backup your data in sd card with file name
pocketmoney_database.db and then you can restore it.
